About me

Hello, I am Nathan Blontrock, 31 years old and I am currently a student at Thomas More Hogeschool.

I have a long history as an audio engineer but after 7 fruitful years and hard work I had seen enough. The tight budgets, insane hours and subjectiveness of the work weren't going to keep me sane anymore. Although audio will forever have a place in my heart I was ready to explore new horizons.

After doing some research I decided to go back to school and immediately settled on the Internet of Things or Programming graduates. These courses kindled my technical side while being more of an exact science; it either works or it doesn't.

I have an appartment in Antwerp where I'm doing a total renovation. I'm doing most of the work myself and am "almost" done now...

I play drums, badminton and love going to concerts, movies, fine restaurants,...
